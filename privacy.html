Privacy Policy

This policy is simple because we collect very little.

Measurements & Data

We use simple, anonymous counts to understand if the space is being visited.

Your Words

If you enter the space and choose to speak, your words are processed to provide a response. We do not store transcripts of your sessions attached to your identity.

Third Parties

We use standard infrastructure providers to host this space. They process data securely but do not have the right to use your personal inputs for their own marketing.

Your Rights

You can leave at any time. Because we store so little, there is often nothing to delete. If you have provided an email or account details, you may request their deletion entirely.

Changes

If we change this policy, it will be to clarify, not to encroach. We will post updates here.
